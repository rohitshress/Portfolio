About Me

The Journey

I am a Grade 11 Science student with a deep fascination for how the world works, from the laws of Physics to the logic of computer code. Born and raised in Nuwakot and currently based in Kathmandu, I balance my rigorous academic life with my passion for building things on the web.

What started as a curiosity about how websites are made has turned into a dedicated hobby. I enjoy the challenge of writing clean CSS and solving complex problems, much like I enjoy deriving mechanics formulas in class.

"In Science, I find the laws of the universe. In Code, I find the power to build my own."

When I am not studying or coding, you can find me exploring the streets with my camera. Photography allows me to see the world through a different lens, which often inspires the visual design of my web projects.
